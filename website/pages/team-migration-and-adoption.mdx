# Team / Migration / Adoption

This section is for people who are moving beyond "what is APW?" and into "how do we adopt or extend it safely?"

<Callout type="warning" emoji="🧱">
APW stays strongest when teams adopt it explicitly. Do not treat migration, compatibility, or rollout work as silent internal refactors.
</Callout>

## The safe adoption pattern

<Steps>
### Choose the rollout scope

Decide whether you are adopting APW for one repo, one pilot, or a wider team effort.

### Confirm the contract

Read the migration, compatibility, and bootstrap material before changing working repos.

### Roll out intentionally

Use one canonical APW model instead of drifting into tool-specific or team-specific forks.
</Steps>

## Core message

APW stays strongest when adoption is explicit and coordinated:

- one canonical framework
- one bootstrap contract
- one validator
- thin compatibility layers
- no silent migrations or tool-specific forks
